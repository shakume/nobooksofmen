Societies of the Night

The loose structure of vampires are as follows:

  1. The Individual: Ultimately, the Vampire cares mostly for themselves. They know and work with others when needed, but most are loners to one degree or another and prefer to work alone or with a small group of 2-3 others (see below). Even when they choose to associate with a cabal (or Nest), their own freedom always comes first.
  2. The Cabal: When Vampires befriend and bond with a group of other vampires (or less often other Mortals or Supernaturals) they claim themselves to be a part of a "Cabal." The Cabal are those who you would risk your health and life for if need be, with the expectation they would do the same for you.
  3. The Nest: A loose, city or regional confederation of Cabals in a given collection of territories is called a Nest. The Nest is typically "ruled" by a single Vampire or a "Leadership Cabal" who dictates the rules of a given area. The Nest is not offical or necessarily enforced, but those individuals or Cabals which threaten the overall safety or hunting abilities of the Nest will be "dealt with" swiftly and brutally.
  4. The Conspiracy: Not technically part of the "hierarchy" structure, but if present the Conspiracy to which a Vampire belongs is wholly secondary to the obligations of the Self, the Cabal, and the Nest. The possible exception is if an individual or Cabal dedicates themselves to a Conspiracy over a Nest, in which case these two ranks are "flipped" with each other.
