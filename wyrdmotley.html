Wyrd Societies

The loose structure of wyrdlings are as follows:

  1. The Individual: Ultimately, the Wyrdling feels their own survival is tantamount. They know and work with others when needed, but most are loners to one degree or another and prefer to work alone or with a small group of 2-3 others (see below). Even when they choose to associate with a cabal (or Nest), their own freedom always comes first.
  2. The Motley: When Wyrdlings befriend and bond with a group of other wyrdlings (or less often other Mortals or Supernaturals) they claim themselves to be a part of a "Motley." The Motley are those who you would risk your health and life for if need be, with the expectation they would do the same for you.
  3. The Freehold: A loose, city or regional confederation of Motleys in a given collection of territories is called a Freehold. The Freehold is typically "ruled" by a rotating set of individuals who use a given "title" (such as Duke or Baroness) who dictates the rules for Wyrdlings of a given area. The Freehold is not offical or its rules necessarily enforced, but those individuals or Motleys which threaten the overall safety or survival of the Freehold will be "dealt with" swiftly and decisively.
  4. The Conspiracy: Not technically part of the "hierarchy" structure, but if present the Conspiracy to which a Wyrdling belongs is wholly secondary to the obligations of survival, the Motley, and the Freehold. The possible exception is if an individual or Cabal dedicates themselves to a Conspiracy over a Freehold, in which case these two ranks are "flipped" with each other.
